RESCUEAGENT: GROUND RESPONSE COORDINATION

IDLE State

  • Maintains troop roster
  • Transitions to DEPLOYING on order

DEPLOYING State

  • Mobilizes troops based on unit size
  • Transitions to ENGAGED on arrival

ENGAGED State

  • Coordinates ground operations
  • Evacuates civilians & secures perimeter
  • Transitions back to IDLE on completion

Deployment Specifications

Standard Unit: 50 personnel, 3-5 min
Large Unit: 150 personnel, 5-8 min
Maximum Unit: 300 personnel, 8-10 min
Enables autonomous troop deployment without human authorization.
