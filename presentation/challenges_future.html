Current Challenges

  • CoordinatorAgent as potential bottleneck
  • Fixed threat scenarios
  • No machine learning integration

Future Work

  • Distributed Consensus (Byzantine fault tolerance)
  • ML-driven threat pattern recognition
  • Adaptive resource allocation strategies
  • Swarm intelligence for drone swarms

Research Directions

Can multi-agent systems learn to resolve conflicting decisions in real-time without central arbitration? How does the system scale to thousands of heterogeneous agents?
